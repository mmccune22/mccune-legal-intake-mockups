Student Loans

Student loans are not priority debts, but they get special treatment. They are presumed to be non-dischargeable, meaning they usually survive a bankruptcy — though in some cases they can be discharged. Your attorney will discuss your options with you if you owe student loans.

Unsecured Debts

Debts not tied to any property — credit cards, medical / dental bills, payday / online loans, personal / family loans, and similar. A rough total is fine.

We will pull your credit reports, but please also upload your most recent statements for these debts to the Unsecured debt statements box on the left. Medical bills no longer appear on credit reports, so it is very important that you upload every medical bill. You will also want to upload any collection letters or notices from attorneys related to these debts.
